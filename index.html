Achievements

One of the best Girls Day school

Carmel Convent School ranked Number 1 in India and Number 1 in the region among the Girls Day School in the EducationWorld India School Rankings 2020-21.

Sports Champions for session 2018-2019

Carmel Convent School Chandigarh was declared overall sports champions for the session 2018-19, by the Chandigarh Sports Department. The students were felicitated by His Excellency the Governor of Punjab Mr. V.P Badnore at a function held in Tagore Theatre.

What's new in the shcool

  • Carmel Convent Awarded at International Women’s Day

    On the occasion of International Women’s Day, Don Bosco Navjeevan Society in collaboration with Chandigarh Urban Festival (CUF) organised an event on March 7th, 2022 at St Stephen’s School, Sector-45-B Chandigarh. Carmel Convent School was honoured at the award ceremony held for ‘Women with Great Contributions to Society’. The awards were presented for work towards encouraging, empowering, inspiring and honouring women and their aspirations in all aspects of their lives. This event was graced with the presence of our esteemed Chief Guest Ms Hargunjit Kaur, IAS, Fr. Reji Tom, Director, Don Bosco Navjeevan Society(DBNS) and other honorary dignitaries including Ms. Shobha Kausar, Ms Sunita Dhawan, AHTU, Adv Neha Ahluwalia, Mrs Shilpa Das and other core members of Child Friendly City Initiative.

  • World Thinking Day Celebrations

    World Thinking Day celebrations were held at the Bharat Scouts and Guides State HQ, Sec 19-C, Chandigarh, on February 22, 2022. This celebration was held to honour the National and State Prize winners with certificates and awards. Guides Kenisha,Vandita, Nyssa, Shine ,Twinkle and Ms Preeti Swami, DOC representing BSG Chandigarh Carmel Convent School, were honoured by Ms. Prabhjot Kaur, State Headquarter Commissioner-cum-DEO (UT Chandigarh) and Ms. Bindu Arora, Dy. DEO (State Treasurer, BS&G), Education Department (UT Chandigarh)
